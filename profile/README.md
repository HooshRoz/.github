# Hoosh Roz

Hoosh Roz is your smart Persian daily life assistant. It fights financial stress with AI-powered budgeting, expense tracking, smart bill reminders, purchase timing advice, savings suggestions, and personalized investment ideas tailored to Iran's inflation reality.
